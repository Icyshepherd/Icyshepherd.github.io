Mileworld Transportation Design

Sep 2024 - Jan 2025
Automotive design course project
  • Mileworld builds a transportation service system composed of vehicles, stations, and an app to provide tourists in Erdaobaihe Town with a travel mode that balances ride comfort and travel information services. Through the app, users can enter their travel plans and mobility needs, choose suitable vehicle types and recommended routes, then board dedicated low-speed enclosed autonomous vehicles at stations set up near key transport hubs and attractions. The vehicles use a modular combination system to allocate capacity precisely, reduce waste of space and energy, and provide private customized service. The app integrates tourism information and official news from multiple platforms, combines them with real-time traffic conditions, and offers the best travel plan suggestions for the moment.

Intelligent Club Recruitment Matching Platform

Mar 2026
Personal vibe coding project, internet product demo
  • This product addresses common problems in university club recruitment such as information asymmetry, cumbersome sign-up procedures, and difficulty in matching interests. It is designed as a student-centered intelligent club recruitment matching platform. The platform combines interest tag selection with an intelligent chatbot Q&A flow, guiding freshmen to express their needs regarding club types, activity content, and preferred modes of participation, so they can receive recommendations that better match their interests and development goals. Clubs can present activity highlights, organizational features, and trial-event information on dedicated pages, helping freshmen understand the atmosphere and actual content more intuitively. After browsing the recommendation results, freshmen can further register for trial activities and lower the barrier to decision-making. Once they have gained a full understanding of a club, users can also sign up directly through the club page, creating a one-stop recruitment experience from interest discovery and intelligent recommendation to deeper understanding and convenient joining.
